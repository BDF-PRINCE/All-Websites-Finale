About the Server

Life SMP is a private Minecraft server where friends come together to build, explore, and have fun. We focus on creating a friendly and engaging environment for all members.

Server Rules

  • No griefing or stealing.
  • No spawn trapping.
  • No hacking or using unfair mods.
  • Be respectful to all players.
  • No spamming in chat.
  • No advertising other servers.
  • No exploiting glitches or bugs.
  • Only build Good looking structures near spawn.
  • Dont make secret bases or bunkers make houses above land(Not in air)
  • Dont steal items from someones house, only ask the to borrow or trade.
  • No offensive builds or signs.
  • Keep chat family-friendly.
  • No PvP without consent.
  • Don't build lag machines.
  • Follow staff instructions.
  • No duplicating items.
  • Report bugs to staff.
  • Don't impersonate staff.
  • No floating trees.
  • Light up your builds to prevent mob spawning.
  • Respect other players' builds and space.
  • Help new players if you can.
  • No x-ray texture packs.
  • Not obeying any one of these can cause PERMANENT OR TEMPORARY BAN!!!
  • Have fun and enjoy the game!
